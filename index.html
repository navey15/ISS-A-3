Exploring New Delhi

Being from New Delhi, I have always been surrounded by the rich cultural heritage and iconic landmarks of the city. Here are some of the most famous places that define the beauty and history of New Delhi:

India Gate

A war memorial and one of the most iconic landmarks in New Delhi, India Gate stands as a tribute to the soldiers who sacrificed their lives for the country.

Red Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Red Fort is a symbol of India's rich history and the site where the Prime Minister hoists the national flag on Independence Day.

Connaught Place

Known as the heart of New Delhi, Connaught Place is a bustling commercial and cultural hub, famous for its colonial architecture and vibrant atmosphere.
